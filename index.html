About Us

The Sweden 🇸🇪 Dog Trainer offers exceptional one on one modern dog training with an emphasis on proven, ethical and science based teaching methods. Fully qualified and with years of experience, all of his work is 100% satisfaction guaranteed. Always done with kindness and reward based, Dean’s work is about empowering owners with knowledge and skillset to achieve the great behaviours you want from your dog. And all done with kind, clear, fun and scientifically based training, making sure training’s a pleasure for you both and your dog. He has worked with dogs from Stockholm to Los Angeles to London and all ages and experience are catered for in a safe, realistic environment (outdoors where possible), helping you transfer your new skills into real life.
